This is padding and margin lesson

Padding Definition: Padding is the space inside an element, between the element’s content (like text or image) and its border. Think of it like the inner cushion around the content. Example: If you have a button with text, padding is the extra space inside the button around the text.
Margin Definition: Margin is the space outside an element, between the element’s border and the next element. Think of it like the outer space around the element. Example: If you want to separate two boxes apart, you use margin.
